               >>>   Example 5 - Talbot Suite DE   <<<

         Application of Talbot's method to solve the PDE equation

			u_t (x,y,t) = u_xx (x,y,t) + u_yy (x,y,t),      0 < x,y < 1,  t>0

	with conditions:
			u(x,y,0+) = x*(x-1) + y*(y-1)
			u(0,y,t) = u(1,y,t) = 4*t + y*(y-1)
			u(x,0,t) = u(x,1,t) = 4*t + x*(x-1)

	The analytical solution is

		u(x,y,t) = 4*t + x*(x-1) + y*(y-1)

	and its Laplace Transform is

		          4    x*(x-1) + y*(y-1)
		U(x,s) = --- + -----------------
		         s^2           s

	with a double pole at 0

	     and abscissa of convergence: sigma0 = 0
