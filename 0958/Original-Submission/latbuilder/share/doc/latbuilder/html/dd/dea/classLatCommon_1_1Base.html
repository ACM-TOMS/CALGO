Detailed Description

This class represents a basis for a lattice.

To compute the length of vectors, one may use either the \(L_1\), the \(L_2\) or the \(L_\infty\) norms. If one uses the \(L_2\) norm and if BScal and NScal are of type double, then the norm could overflow if the components of the basis vectors are larger than \(\approx 2^{500}\). In that case, NScal may be chosen as RR. If one uses the \(L_1\) or the \(L_\infty\) norms, NScal may be chosen the same as BScal.
