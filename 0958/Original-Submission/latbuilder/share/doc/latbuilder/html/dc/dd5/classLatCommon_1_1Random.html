Detailed Description

This class generates random numbers (in fact pseudo-random numbers).

The generator used is the 64-bits generator LFSR258 from L'Ecuyer [14] with period length near \(2^{258}\) for 64-bits machines, and the 32-bits generator LFSR113 from L'Ecuyer [14] with period length near \(2^{113}\) on 32-bits machines. Thus the random numbers generated will be different on 32-bits and 64-bits machines.
